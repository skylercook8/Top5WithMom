Top 5 Moments With My Mom

My inspiration for this wesbite came from the fact that I really miss my mom right now and was scrolling through photos of us. Since I was already looking through photos, I decided to make this wesbite including the photos!

Start scrolling!

1. Mom and Daughter Trip to Florida!

This was when JUST my mom and I took off to Florida without my dad or brother and it was THE BEST! We rented a convertable, ate the yummiest food, had the most perfect beach days, and it was PERFECT!

2. Cheer Competitions

I was a competitive cheerleader for 13 years and had ENDLESS cheer competitions. Lots of good memories with my mom come from those cheer competitions becuase she came to every single one, except for when she was pregnant with my brother. My mom always cheered me on and made sure she was in the front to see me and they are such special memories!

3. Wetsuit Adventure

My mom always books family trips to the beach, but during the off seasons so its cheaper and less crowded. This trip, we were in Nags Head during Christmas and the water was freezing, but my mom and I wanted to hop in SO BAD. To do this, we rented wet suits and swam in the ocean and it was so different, something we've never done before, and such a good time!

4. Tubing

Again with the ourdoor activites, my mom always KILLED these trips. This was at a local spot along the James River where we tube down the river for about three hours. My mom brings lots of snacks and it has now became a family tradition that we do every year!

5. Snuck a Dog Home

My mom and I were wanting a dog FOREVER, but my dad always said no because he doesn't like dogs. We had gave up on asking until that cutie little yorkie showed up on my moms Facebook page. We decided he needed to be ours and we would ask for forguvness instead of permission this time. My dad actually loves the dog and we are so happy we decided to sneak him home.
